Eloquent Research

Building language systems
that work outside the lab

Research is at the heart of Eloquent Labs, and we use our research as the core technology in Eloquent's products. Our NLP research focuses on areas with natural applications to conversational AI: state management for task-oriented dialog, novel neural models semantic similarity, new models and datasets for reading comprehension, and more.

Eloquent's research extends beyond NLP to Human Computer Interaction, crowdsourcing, and creating reliable, low-latency distributed systems for real-time chat.

We believe that great products are driven by great research, and great research should be applied to problems to create great products.
